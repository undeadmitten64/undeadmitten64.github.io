Counter Strike 2

by Valve

9.4 / 10.0

An evergreen title, and probably my favorite FPS. CS2 is a glowing example of how good a game can be made by it's community (like most valve games). Counter Strike has satisfying and responsive gunplay, although there were a few hiccups at the start of CS2's beta, I think the game is feeling good again.

Your Only Move Is HUSTLE

by Ivy Sly

8.9 / 10.0

An incredibly in-depth and unique strategy fighting game, based around predicting your opponents moves, and rewarding you with a dopamine-inducing replay of the full speed fight at the end of the match. This game somehow allows itself to be very fun but also incredibly frustrating, as one mistake can lead to your opponent getting a 2-minute long combo on you, where you sit and do virtually nothing. Maybe it's my fault for maining robot.
